    Introduction

    Air Test Pro is my personal online business that I am growing. I provided detailed air testing data that is used to decipher what the best way is to clean your home. Unlike all the people that have allergies like myself, I have found many people with a variety of health issues like COPD and lung disease that need this information. These visitors don't know what to make of arbitrary data. I am telling and showing them how I test and how I compute the data into information that provides a solution for them.

    I am not just a person creating an informational product. I have severe allergies that I have dealth with since birth. I know how important it is for people to get quality help through information and that is why I decided to make this website. Finding the best solution for each individual person is very hard and I want to make that process easier.

    My Work

    Before I made this website I owned "www.best-air-purifiers-reviews.com", which I did a pretty poor job on for 3 years. It was basic HTML and CSS with no thrills. Now that I am learning new skills, I will be implementing and re-implementing new technologies (Javascript + MEAN Stack). I decided to start off using HTML, CSS and Bootstrap to make basis of my website.

    I spent most of my time working on a single web application for the website. It is located on the bottom of the homepage. In theory it is simple, I provide the visitor with a set of questions and when they select one they are sent to another list of questions that build upon the first question. I started off trying a switch statement, but it didn't work as well as I would like. I decided to do this using a more elegant solution using Javascript. I structured my Javascript file in "Levels". Each level has the variety of questions and/or answers for that level.

    The real heart of the web app is the output options available at certain steps. You could reach a final step that would give you text information, a picture a product with a buy now link or a link for one of my personal products. The information I provide in this web app is free and it allows the visitors to get a good idea of where their issues occur and how they can solve them. I will also be presenting this web app to my Harvard CS50 (C Programming) course in a 2 minute video. It will be my final project for the class.
